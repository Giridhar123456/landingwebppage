KUNTALA WATERFALL

Adilabad is a small town in the state of Telangana that is famous for the beautiful Kunatala waterfalls located amidst lush forests. Originally known as Edulapuram, this place is for nature lovers and history buffs....

KANAKAI WATERFALL

Kanakai Waterfalls located in Girnur village, Telangana is an impassable 30 feet cascade of water stream descending from a cliff into a large pool at the bottom of the falls....

BAGOTHA WATERFALL

Also called the “Niagara of Telangana”, Bogatha Waterfalls is an eye pleasing and the second largest waterfall located in the state of Telangana....

Gyatri waterfall

Gayatri Falls is a beautiful cascading waterfall over River Kadem in the Adilabad district of Telangana. Colloquially known as Gadidha Gundam or Mukti Gundam, the waterfalls Tarnam Khurd Village.

MALLELA THERTHAM

Nestled in the state of Telangana, Mallela Theertham is a hidden gem untouched by industrialization and bountiful in scenic beauty. Falling from a height of about 150 feet, this waterfall is quite a sight to see....

ETHIPOTHALA WATERFALL

Ethipothala Falls is a 70 feet high river cascade, which is a tributary of River Krishna joining on its right bank.

POCHERA WATERFALL

The falls are located at a distance of 10 Kms from Neredikonda village between Nirmal & Adilabad...

LAKE!!!

At a distance of 10 km from Gudur Bus Stand, 51 km from Warangal, 88 km from Khammam Bus Station and 200 km from Hyderabad, Bheemuni Paadam Waterfalls is a picturesque waterfall located at Gudur in Warangal District of Telangana.
